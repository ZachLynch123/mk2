  • About Us

    GetDocsNow.com was created by Real Estate brokers who wanted a better way to obtain resale documents. Joe Russo was driving across Vegas one day to get the resale documents for a real estate transaction he was working on. He drove all the way to the other end of the valley to deliver them and thought: “there has to be a better way”. And this brilliant idea turned into a business plan in 1999. We believe that our background in real estate makes our site more functional and user friendly than any other service out there.

    In 1999 The Russo Group set out to build an online utility website to provide a variety of document disclosures. The creators of GetDocsNow.com , The Russo Group, searched for the best programmers and online engineers to put together this outstanding service. GetDocsNow.com has continued to thrive and grow since its inception and expand into the robust website it is today. The best in the community management industry.

    The Russo Group and GetDocsNow.com strive to provide the best experience possible for both our clients and our users.

    For security and protection we use Authorize.net to manage our transactions & help prevent fraud. You can have confidence our transactions are being processed according to the highest security standards.

    It is our commitment to keep expanding and improving the services for the GetDocsNow.com clients.
